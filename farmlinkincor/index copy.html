FarmLink Site Plan

Course Project: Individual Website Plan Document

Site Name

FarmLink — The name represents a digital platform that connects farmers to buyers, neighbors, and service providers. It reflects the mission of creating a link between producers and the market while promoting visibility for agricultural products.

Optional domain: farmlinkhub.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an online space for farmers to showcase their agricultural products and services to potential buyers in their neighborhood and beyond. The site helps reduce post-harvest losses by giving farmers visibility, offering contact forms for direct orders, and sharing best practices in local farming communities.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find nearby farmers offering fresh vegetables or grains?
  • How can I contact a farmer to purchase produce directly from their farm?

Color Scheme

The selected colors represent freshness, growth, and trust — reflecting the agricultural theme of the project.

  • Primary Color: #3B7A57 – Used for headings and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #F2E8CF – Used for background and contrast areas.

Typography

Primary Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif – used for headings and navigation.

Secondary Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – used for paragraph and body text.
